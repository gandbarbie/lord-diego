Onion Services: Educational Overview

Onion services (`.onion`) keep both client and server identities concealed inside the Tor network. They are useful for secure whistleblower portals, sensitive support channels, and censorship-resistant publishing.

  • No public DNS exposure for service endpoints.
  • Mutual anonymity by design (client and host).
  • Best practice: segment hosting, keys, and admin access paths.
